Welcome

This portal helps in reuniting the lost children with their families. We can get going by first registering the lost child in our database by clicking on the button 'Register' and filling out the form. Then we need to generate encodings for the images stored in the datatset by clicking on the button 'Train". Finally we can check by uploading an image of a missing child by either testing directly on the backend or by using the Android app designed for this purpose. Let's do the good deed to reunite the missing children with their family!
